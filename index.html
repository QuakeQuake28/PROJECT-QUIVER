Enhanced Dataset & Classification

This comprehensive visualization showcases the highest magnitude earthquake recorded each calendar day during the selected month and year, spanning our complete 2020-2025 dataset. Each marker represents the most significant seismic event for that specific date in Philippine Standard Time, providing unprecedented insight into 6 years of seismic patterns.

Advanced Data Processing: Our dataset undergoes rigorous filtering to ensure only the most significant daily events are displayed. Magnitude ranges follow international seismological standards, while the Philippines uses the PHIVOLCS Earthquake Intensity Scale (PEIS) for intensity measurements, developed specifically for local geological conditions by the Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology.
